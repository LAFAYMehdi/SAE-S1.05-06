Atos' Environmental Commitment

Carbon Neutrality and Emission Reduction Goals

Towards a zero-carbon company by 2039

Atos has committed to achieving carbon neutrality by 2039, ahead of the deadlines set by the Paris Agreement. The company is implementing strategies to reduce its greenhouse gas emissions, including optimizing its operations and reducing its energy footprint. This includes the use of renewable energy for its infrastructure, as well as carbon offset programs such as reforestation and energy transition projects.

Eco-Friendly Data Centers and Renewable Energy

Improving energy efficiency and promoting low-carbon cloud

Data centers, which consume a significant amount of energy, are the focus of sustainability initiatives at Atos. The company aims to power 100% of its data centers with renewable energy by 2025. Innovative solutions are being implemented to maximize energy efficiency, including the use of natural cooling systems and low-energy-consuming equipment.

Promoting Circular Economy and Reducing Electronic Waste

Responsible recycling and sustainable management of IT equipment

Atos adopts a circular economy approach in managing its IT equipment, ensuring that end-of-life devices are either reused or recycled responsibly. This strategy aims to limit the production of electronic waste, which is a major source of pollution in the tech industry.

Biodiversity Actions and Carbon Offset Projects

Protecting biodiversity through sustainable environmental initiatives

Atos is committed to supporting biodiversity through carbon offset projects, including concrete initiatives such as reforestation in vulnerable areas. These projects aim to offset CO2 emissions while preserving and restoring natural ecosystems.
